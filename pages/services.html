Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and treatments

Our dermatologists conduct thorough consultations to assess your skin condition, medical history, and aesthetic goals. We also offer our AI symptom checker for preliminary guidance. Based on this comprehensive evaluation, we recommend the most suitable treatment plan for your specific needs.
Medical dermatology treatments for conditions like acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer are typically covered by most insurance plans. Cosmetic procedures are generally not covered. We accept most major insurance providers and offer flexible payment options for cosmetic treatments.
Our Watson Assistant AI has been trained on extensive dermatological data and provides highly accurate preliminary assessments. However, it's designed to supplement, not replace, professional medical consultation. We always recommend following up with our dermatologists for definitive diagnosis and treatment.
Your first visit includes a comprehensive consultation, medical history review, and thorough skin examination. Our dermatologist will discuss your concerns, explain findings, and recommend appropriate treatment options. We'll also provide detailed information about any proposed procedures and answer all your questions.
Results vary by treatment type: Botox typically lasts 3-4 months, dermal fillers 6-18 months depending on the type, laser treatments show progressive improvement over several months, and chemical peels provide lasting results with proper skincare maintenance. We'll discuss expected duration during your consultation.
